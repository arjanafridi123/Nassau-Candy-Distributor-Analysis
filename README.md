# Product Line Profitability & Margin Performance Analysis for Nassau Candy Distributor 

# Overview

An interactive business analytics project built using Python, Pandas, Plotly, and Streamlit to analyze product profitability, gross margin performance, revenue concentration, division performance, and cost structure across Nassau Candy Distributor's product portfolio.

The project transforms raw sales and profitability data into actionable business insights that support:

- product profitability analysis,
- gross margin performance evaluation,
- revenue and profit concentration analysis,
- division performance assessment,
- cost optimization opportunities,
- product portfolio optimization,
- seasonal revenue trend analysis, and
- data-driven business decision-making.

# 🎯 Problem → Solution → Impact

# ⚠️ Problem

Nassau Candy Distributor manages a diverse product portfolio across multiple divisions and regions. However, relying solely on raw sales data makes it difficult to identify the most profitable products, evaluate gross margin performance, detect cost inefficiencies, and understand revenue concentration risks. This limits strategic decision-making for pricing, product portfolio optimization, and long-term profitability.

# ⚙️ Solution

Developed an interactive business analytics dashboard and exploratory data analysis framework to examine:

* Product revenue and gross profit performance
* Gross margin analysis across products and divisions
* Cost versus sales relationships
* Revenue and profit concentration using Pareto Analysis
* Product dependency and concentration risk
* Cost renegotiation opportunities
* Low-performing products for discontinuation review
* Monthly revenue, profit, and margin trends
* Division-level profitability and operational performance

# 📈 Impact

The analysis transforms transactional sales data into actionable business intelligence, enabling management to:

* Identify the most profitable products and divisions
* Reduce dependence on a small number of high-performing products
* Optimize pricing and supplier cost negotiations
* Improve product portfolio decisions
* Increase profitability through margin-focused strategies
* Monitor business performance using an interactive Streamlit dashboard
* Support data-driven strategic planning and financial decision-making

# 📌 Key KPIs

| KPI                                  | Description                                                                   |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Total Revenue**                    | Total sales revenue generated across all products                             |
| **Total Gross Profit**               | Total profit earned after deducting product costs                             |
| **Average Gross Margin (%)**         | Average percentage of revenue retained as gross profit                        |
| **Profit Per Unit**                  | Average gross profit generated for each unit sold                             |
| **Margin Volatility**                | Variation in monthly gross margin indicating pricing and cost stability       |
| **Top Product Revenue Contribution** | Percentage of total revenue generated by the highest-performing products      |
| **Top Product Profit Contribution**  | Percentage of total gross profit generated by the highest-performing products |
| **Highest Revenue Division**         | Business division generating the greatest total revenue                       |
| **Highest Margin Division**          | Business division achieving the highest average gross margin                  |
| **Monthly Revenue Trend**            | Revenue performance across different months to identify seasonal patterns     |

# 💡 Business Insights Generated

The dashboard helps uncover:

* The products contributing the highest revenue, gross profit, and gross margin.
* Revenue and profit concentration risks using Pareto Analysis.
* Business divisions driving overall sales and profitability.
* Low-margin and high-cost products requiring pricing or supplier cost optimization.
* Products suitable for portfolio optimization or discontinuation review.
* Monthly revenue, gross profit, and gross margin trends to identify seasonal business patterns.
* Product dependency risks caused by reliance on a small number of high-performing products.
* Actionable insights that support pricing strategy, inventory planning, product portfolio management, and data-driven business decision-making.
