# Product Line Profitability & Margin Performance Analysis for Nassau Candy Distributor 

# Overview

An interactive business analytics project built using Python, Pandas, Plotly, and Streamlit to analyze product profitability, gross margin performance, revenue concentration, division performance, and cost structure across Nassau Candy Distributor's product portfolio.

The project transforms raw sales and profitability data into actionable business insights that support:

product profitability analysis,
gross margin performance evaluation,
revenue and profit concentration analysis,
division performance assessment,
cost optimization opportunities,
product portfolio optimization,
seasonal revenue trend analysis, and
data-driven business decision-making.
